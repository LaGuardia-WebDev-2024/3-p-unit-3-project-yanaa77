Recipe #1 Crepes

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: about 40 pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity Unit
all purpose flour 350 grams
salt 1/2 teaspoon
sugar 3 tablespoon
baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
eggs 2 egg
milk 800 ml
melted butter 5 tablespoons

Let's cook!

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, sugar and baking soda. Add eggs, milk, cream and melted butter.

Step 2: Use a whisk to mix the batter until smooth. The consistency of the batter should be a bit thicker than cream with no lumps.

Step 3: Heat a pan over high heat. Pour a small amount of sunflower oil in the middle. Quickly, tilt the pan from side to side while pouring, spreading evenly in a circle.

Step 3: Acting quickly, tilt the pan from side to side while pouring, forming a circle with the batter. Flip the pancake over, after the surface looks porous and sides are golden. Fry for half of the time you fried the first side. Repeat with the rest of the batter.

Blini(crepes)

Blini are a big part of Slavic culture. During the Eastern Slavic folk holiday Maslenitsa, they are eaten in large quantities, shared with friends, and celebrated as the highlight of the week before Orthodox Easter. You can fill them with anything: tvorog, honey, apples, nuttela, condensed milk, sour cream, caviar,...
